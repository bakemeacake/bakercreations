Design

Logos

The image that represents a company or business. Logos rely on images, can be represented by a mascot and usually include text; it depends on organization preference.

Icons

A group of relating images arranged usually in grid form or some kind of compositional layout. These images tend to have a them and are simple representations of the original thing.

Infographics

Visual representations of, often, statistical information. Infographics take research findings and make them easy to understand at a glance as well as add interest.

Product Redesign

Sometimes packaged products need a face-lift or a make-over. Redesigns usually include a change in font, color scheme, images and composition. Occasionally, even the package shape can change.

Websites

Extend from designing each page layout to choosing the color of the text. Each choice is important in finding the balance between beauty and functionality.

Posters

Could also be grouped with digital ads, because not much is on paper anymore. Advertisements, event flyers etc. arranged to attract attention and invite interest.

Social Media & Articles

Companies and businesses ought to have a cohesive look and voice that speaks through their social media as well as their brand. Using attractive visuals for articles and videos is imperative in keeping that consistent feel and attracting readers.
